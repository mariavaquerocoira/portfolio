Maria Vaquero Coira is a designer and art director from Madrid with over 15 years of experience. She works across identity, branding, packaging, interactive, and advertising, developing visual solutions for international brands. She has worked in different countries and across a range of environments, including in-house teams, design studios, and advertising agencies. She is currently a Senior Art Director at Apple in California.

She can be contacted at mariavaquerocoira@gmail.com
